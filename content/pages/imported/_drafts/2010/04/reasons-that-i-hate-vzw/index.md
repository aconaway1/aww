date: 2010-04-21

---

Here are some complaints I have against Verizon Wireless.  If I could get coverage with any other provider, I definitely would not be a customer of thiers.  This list is ever-changing.

- Their "New Every Two" program is terrible.  Phones break and/or wear out normally in about a year.  Two years is way too long to wait for a replacement.
- [It costs less to send data to the International Space Station than it does to send a text message.](http://www.physorg.com/news129793047.html)
- They're data network is slow.  I dread the day when they get the iPhone.
- Their phones' firmware often disables or cripples features of a phone.  For example, I can't use the GPS in my phone without buying their app.
- Selling ringtones and games at ridiculous prices is insulting.
- Their choice of phones is limited and every-changing.  You can buy a new phone one day, and it will be discontinued later that week.
- They have absolutely no QA department for their phones.  I haven't heard a single person in the last two years say they've never had a problem with their phone's hardware or software.
- Does anyone have a decent phone whose battery lasts longer than 12 hours?
- I love it when my phone calls people randomly while I'm talking.
- I also love having to take the battery out of the phone when the "End" button no longer works.
- Paying full price for a phone is a ripoff.  A basic cell phone is not worth $600 retail.
- With my bottom-of-the-barrel voice plan, a data plan, and my employee discount, I pay over $100 per month.  That's $1200 per year for basic service.
- The program that gives the main number on the account a new phone every year was discontinued in February.
- The local store had no idea why I couldn't get a new phone after a year.  No one had told them that that program was canceled.
- Bait-and-switch #1:  While logged in to their site, I'm offered $79 for phone X only to have that price only available for new lines.
- Bait-and-switch #2:  While logged in to their site, I'm shown an upgrade button by my line and a graphic beside my wife's line saying she can upgrade next year.  That tells me they have a special for me.  Nope, they just want me to pay full retail.
- They no longer support the phones they sell.  According to the local VZW salesman, I need to contact the manufacturer for support on the phone they gave me 8 months ago.
- Their various sales teams have different information.  The local stores says that the annual program is still in affect.  The toll free number says it's canceled.
- They're salesmen no longer ever try to sell phones any more.  Even when I come in for one, they are too lazy to try.
