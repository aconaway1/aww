Hi, I'm Aaron Conaway — a network engineer who occasionally puts words on the internet.

I've been working in networking for a long time, mostly in enterprise and service provider environments. Over the years I've built things with Cisco IOS, Juniper Junos, FortiOS, and whatever else gets thrown at me. These days I spend a lot of time thinking about network automation, which mostly means I write Python and try to convince others that [NetBox](https://github.com/netbox-community/netbox) should be the source of truth for everything.

I am not a developer. I will remind you of this frequently.

I was a Cisco Champion and a Juniper Ambassador for a time and have had the privilege of attending [Tech Field Day](https://techfieldday.com/) and [Network Field Day](https://techfieldday.com/event/nfd29/) events as a delegate. I've also appeared on the [Packet Pushers](https://packetpushers.net/) podcast.

My code is on [GitHub](https://github.com/aconaway1). It works, probably.
