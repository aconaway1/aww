I'm all set up to go to Cisco Live in Orlando this year.  Good thing, too, since I couldn't make it to San Diego last time. It'll be a great and fun time as usual, and I'm quite excited.

As it turns out, [ARRL Field Day](http://www.arrl.org/field-day) happens to be the weekend leading up to the festivities.  I've been in contact with the local Orlando club, and they say the attendees are more than welcome to join them.  They are meeting at the [City of Orlando Emergency Operations Center](https://maps.google.com/maps?q=110+Andes+Ave+in+Orlando&client=ubuntu&channel=cs&hnear=110+Andes+Ave,+Orlando,+Orange,+Florida+32803&gl=us&t=h&z=16), which is about 20 minutes away from the Convention Center.
