I can't believe I stuck with something for 2 years.  I usually get a month in before I get bored and give up.

Anyway, 2 years ago this month, I wrote a terribly-written article on [The Principle of Least Privilege](http://aconaway.com/2007/08/09/the-principle-of-least-privilege/ "AConaway.com -- The Principle of Least Privilege").  Since then, though, my writing has improved (at least, I think it has), the topics have broadened, and I've picked up some faithful readers (thanks, Mom!).

In all seriousness, I'd like to thank the blogging community for supporting, commenting, and linking.  I would also like to thank the readers for keeping the hits up, keeping the comments rolling, and contributing more and more content and ideas.

The next time you have your preferred beverage, raise your glass in thanks to yourself and to another 2 years of success!
